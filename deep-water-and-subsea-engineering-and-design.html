Deep-water and Subsea Engineering and Design

Deepwater and Subsea engineering is clearly the new frontier for Oil and Gas exploration. While its future prospects looks promising, we are also well versed with the inherent engineering complexities associated with Deepwater activities. We at Oceanergy understand that to solve these issues, we require an innovative and practicable approach. Being a risk and capex intensive industry we give paramount importance to safety and ease of project execution.
Our DEEP WATER Core Competency are:

MOORING AND RISER DESIGN
• Mooring Design and Analysis – Spread, Turret (SPM), SBM
• Riser/Umbilical – Complete design, installation, deployment, wet-park recovery and In-place analysis (VIV)



SUBSEA ENGINEERING
• Pipeline Design Engineering, Transportation and Installation analysis
• Subsea structure transportation, installation and deployment analysis



FPSO TOPSIDE MODULE
• Structural Design and Engineering
• Module transportation and Installation engineering
• Offshore heavy-lift
