Overview

OCEANERGY was established in 2012 with a vision to be a leading service provider in Engineering Design Solutions and Consultancy Services for Oil and Gas Industry.
Team Oceanergy comprises of stalwarts from diverse fields such as engineering, project management and Operations backgrounds, a cohesive unit to provide the highest quality of Engineering Design Solution and Consultancy services for the oil and gas industry. A well-balanced blend of Veterans and Youth, the guidance of experienced personnel coupled with the dynamism of our young members enable us to deliver most practicable yet innovative solutions.Our team, with its engineering proficiency, management skills, field experience and in-depth knowledge of oil and gas industry, provides a strategic mixture of technical and hands-on expertise to meet our clients’ needs and requirements in not only the safest, but also a cost effective manner.
We are committed to providing the highest level of engineering & design solution and consultancy services. We are a quality-driven company and have stringent quality norms to ensure that our deliverables exceed our clients" expectation.We are committed to establishing a standard of excellence in design, technology and solutions. Our resolute commitment to never compromise on our driving principles is the key to our continuous success.

Key Personnel

Mr. G. NATARAJAN is the senior most member of our team and has over 37 years of Work Experience. He retired from O.N.G.C as Executive Director. He currently is our advisor and mentor. During his illustrious career, which spanned almost 4 decades, he has held responsible positions with ONGC and its overseas wing OVL and spearheaded many multi-billion dollar projects – both onshore and offshore. He was involved in the complete spectrum from EPIC to O&M of multi-million dollar assets.These assets included Offshore and Land Rigs, Pipeline, Onshore and Offshore Production Facility, Gas terminal and Gas Power Generation station.. His holistic knowledge and guidance enables us to provide practicable and cost effective engineering solution. He is a very revered person in the Oil and Gas Industry.

Mr. A. S. Prasad is the head of our Subsea Field and Pipeline division. He has experience of over 15 years and has worked in various countries such as India, UK, Argentina, Chile and Nigeria. He has Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur and MBA from Kingston Business School. He is also a Chartered Engineer Registered with Institution of Mechanical Engineers UK. He is experienced in crude oil, petroleum products pipeline design - both on land and offshore. He has authored the paper on “Flow Assurance in Flexible Pipelines by Using Eductors” in the International Pipeline Conference held in Rio de Janeiro. His division provides engineering solution for Subsea Field FEED, Pipe Line design and Flow assurance – transient and steady state -single and multiphase flow.

Mr. R. K. Verma is the Founder of Oceanergy and is the head of Mooring, Riser and Hydrodynamics division. He has over 7 years of work experience across the Globe – South East Asia, Middle East and Germany. He has Bachelor’s degree in Ocean Engineering and Naval Architecture and Master’s in Offshore Engineering from IIT Kharagpur. His division provides engineering solutions for risk intensive activities such as Float-Off / Float-Over, Deep Water and Ultra Deep water riser Deployment and Installation, Offshore Heavy Lift, Transportation & installation, Turret and Deep Sea mooring solutions for Offshore Rigs and Vessels. He has considerable experience in Vessel Vetting and as Marine Warranty Surveyor.

Mr. A. K. Jha is the head of our Structural and Hydrostatics division. He has experience of over 6 years working in various regions such Middle East, South East Asia and India. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Ocean Engineering and Naval Architecture from IIT Kharagpur and Master’s in Sub-sea Engineering from University of Aberdeen UK. He has diverse skill set and is well conversant with Subsea Engineering which he jointly oversea with Mr. A.S Prasad. His division deals with complete spectrum of structural analysis from – basic finite element analysis to complex impact and collision analysis.
